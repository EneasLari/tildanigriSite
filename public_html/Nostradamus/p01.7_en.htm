1.7 The time lines and destiny


The task of organizing the material and presenting it to the world … The arrangement of the quatrains in some kind of chronological order has been a big job because they often refer to more than one event and the events are in different time periods. Nostradamus himself pointed out that the number of years into the future he was seeing had no limit. He could see, he said, not to the end of time but to the end of Earth. Some of the quatrains were difficult to date because he also foresaw an even more terrible time in the far future when the changes in the earth would be very drastic.

He says it’s hard for him to spot the location because of the turmoil of the time lines at this point in time; since we are nearing close to the events to happen it causes an effect somewhat like a thunderstorm in the time lines.  From his standpoint, an event that will happen in 30-35 years seems so close, that the time we are talking about it and the time when the event happens, practically coincide. When he sees that point in time it’s like a storm cloud that’s building upon itself and flashing out lightening in all directions.

Let me describe to you what the landscape of time, in general looks like. Imagine if you will a gigantic plane that is the purest, blackest velvet you can ever imagine. Across this plane there are lines of neon bright light. The time lines, look like a gigantic emission-line spectrum which is a very small portion of the entire landscape in general. This is just a section of the timescape I'm looking at. There are many possible realities, or possible futures. These lines go along neatly, nicely and orderly, and suddenly they all run together into a central point. That's what I call a nexus. The Anti-Christ is at a main nexus, and no matter which time line you go down, he's going to be involved. The Great Genius is at another nexus. He's like a bright light on the horizon.

These two are so involved in our future that there's no way to get around them, but we can lessen the possible disastrous effects. But there are other paths where various events could be avoided. Time can go in many different directions and there could be different results according to decisions made along the way.

You must believe it, it really is possible to avoid some of the so-called "worse-case scenarios" he’s seen and help lessen the destruction. One example is the "assassination" of Pope John Paul II for whom Nostradamus is categorical! As we already know, there was an attempted assassination against him, from which although he was in serious danger, he managed to survive.

"The dropping of the atomic bomb had an extremely shocking effect on the time lines", Nostradamus says. Imagine if you will a gigantic plane that is the purest, blackest velvet you can ever imagine. Across this plane there are lines of neon bright light. It looks like a gigantic emission-line spectrum in astronomy, which is a very small portion of the entire timescape in general. Each one of these lines represents a time line, a possible reality, or a possible future.

There are many possible futures. These lines go along neatly, nicely and orderly, and suddenly they all run together into a central point. This is what I call "nexus". They all run together, and at the point where they all meet it looks like a gigantic explosion - a big blast of light frozen as its most expanded point. And instead of being able to see the nexus, you see this explosion that has been frozen instead.

In so many of the various realities the world did not survive the inventing of the atomic bomb. In this world, the scientists did not know whether or not it would set off a chain reaction and make every atom in the world explode, thus destroying the world. That was one of their fears. Well, in alternate realities that did indeed happen because of the way these alternate realities were structured. As you know, when it comes to a point where a problem can go one way or the other, it actually goes both ways, but only one way is expressed in your reality. The other way is expressed in another reality.

Time and space are an illusion or a third dimensional creation. Time is meaningless in his place. We should not let the vicissitudes of time affect us too drastically since time is really not as rigid as we think it is. Time is actually very flexible. Once you release that, you get into the pure essence of the vibration, and it’s like fine-tuning a particular channel. He has the ability to release time and space and see many parallel times happening at once. It’s like the cubicles of a hive.
