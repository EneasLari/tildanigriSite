3.23 The Cabal

The «Cabal» is a non-political group of people; they are the ones who are really in control. This organization has been in existence for several generations. They actually control the world history without being either presidents or kings. A hint to their existence is to trace the family histories of the banking powers and the money powers in the world. Banking families and other commodity-related families, such as families into gold mines and diamond mines, leather, tins and such as this; the basic colonial barons associated with the European world empires who started their families’ fortunes by exploiting the raw materials of the Third World nations.

This group of puppeteers will be somewhat like a military junta, but not exactly. They are behind the scenes pulling the strings or the figures on stage and changing the scenery as needed. They manipulate the economy to cause the unemployment rate, or inflation to rise or fall at their whim, stock prices, food growth and money, precious gems; to be able to strangle a market in one area and free flow in another area, control population through wars and disease.


Without either foot or hand, with strong and sharp teeth
through the crowd to the fortified harbor and the elder born.
Near the gates, treacherous, he crosses over;
the moon shines but little, great pillage. (ΙΙ-58)

These «the puppet masters» appear not to have any political maneuvering power. They have no «foot or hand» to push people around with. They’re working for their own ends but they’re very clever in disguising it. They hold positions that appear to be relatively minor, like advisors and under-secretaries and such, but are key positions for their power. At first when the Anti-Christ comes along, they feel he’s just a new, dynamic, youthful leader from the Middle East they can use to help unite that part of the world and get it under their power.


But the Anti-Christ ends up turning the tables on them. He will have them murdered, not realizing they were actually helping him. «The moon shines but little, great pillage», refers to the fact that these people are out of touch with their psychic and intuitive selves, so the moon shines very little in their lives. The moon represents the celestial body that is in charge of psychic things, so to speak.

The partner, solitary but married, will be mitred,
the return, fighting will cross over the Tuileries.
By five hundred one traitor will be ennobled,
Narbonne & Saulce, we will have oil for knives. (ΙΧ-34)

They decide who will be in power of the various governments, so they «ennoble» people; the traders – the «traitors» - are the ones who are being puppeted by the Cabal. They have been made powerful by them trading weapons with the Middle East in exchange for crude oil –«oil for knives» so to speak.

One of them is in the United States.


He will rise high over his wealth, more to the right,
he will remain seated on the square stone;
towards the south placed at the window,
a crooked staff in his hand, his mouth sealed. (V-75)

There is a man in the United States who is very wealthy to the point that anything he wants done can get done instantaneously. This man will be well known and famous because of his wealth, but his true mission in life will be secret, for this man will be a fanatic of sorts. He will be involved with such organizations as the American Nazi Party and the Ku Klux Klan.

The phrase «the crooked staff» represents the burning crosses of the Ku Klux Klan and the swastika of the Nazi party. This man’s sole ambition in life is towards the overthrow of the American government as it is presently constituted in the 20th century. This man naturally enough will also be involved with politics.

But even though his main ambition is to change the form of the American government, he has to stay low-key in politics so that he may continue spinning his webs of power and continue making new contracts and expanding his range of influence. The groundwork that he lays will come to fruition at the time of the troubles caused by the Anti-Christ. At this present time those who follow him know who he is. But the other people don’t know the danger he poses for he has been very sly, very careful. «He will remain seated on the square stone», means that he will be in the center of the entire organization, but he won’t be the figure of power that people see. He will have a puppet, someone who will appear to be the one in power, but he will be pulling the strings. He will have a figurehead, but he will remain seated behind, on the square stone that is, in the center of the organization. «His mouth sealed» would mean that he is secret. The phrase «toward the south at the window», means that due to political beliefs, what open activities he does indulge in and the mayhem he likes to cause is more often to appear in the southern part of the country where such mayhem has been somewhat traditional.
 No one’s going to know who he is until he comes to light at the time of the Anti-Christ.


The following is the description of a horrible experiment having to do with genetics, which is of their own action:

In the year 1999, and seven months
from the sky will come the great King of Terror.
He will bring back to life the great king of the Mongols.
Before and after War reigns happily. (Χ- 72)

It’s some sort of joint effort between America, Russia, Japan and some European countries. The person who is in overall charge of this secret project of genetic manipulation or cloning – «the great King of Terror» - is so powerful he’s able to affect and to influence policy decisions made in various countries. They will try to breed back some of the earlier, less civilized, fiercer types of human beings still smart but very cunning and strong. These unfortunate people are used in fighting to see how much better they do than ordinary soldiers.

There are also nine scientists involved that will be known to the world at large, and will be recognized by the initials «K, T, L». They will be isolated, working alone unto themselves and so they will not be aware of the development of the wars of anything of his sort - «separated from judgment and advice» (I-81) - developing super weapons such as the ones who developed nuclear weapons during World War II.

Nine will be set apart from the human flock,
separated from judgment and advice.
Theirfateistobedividedastheydepart.
K. Th. L. dead, banished and scattered. (Ι-81)

As a consequence, their side loses and the winning side finds out who they are. Some of them will meet with very horrible deaths.

During a time of great chaos in the world, the numbers of these unknown controllers will dwindle from seven to three. This will be as a result of four dying which causes a loosening or a slipping of power that is not lost, but is harder to grasp, to pull back and keep. Increasing population is their major problem. Of course the four members will be replaced, becoming a group of seven again. It hasn’t been the same seven people controlling the world for the past hundred years. It is an inheritance.

The seven branches will be reduced to three,
the elder ones will be surprised by death.
Two will be attracted towards fratricidal (strife):
the conspirators will die while asleep. (VI-11)

Later on, during the time of the Anti-Christ, two armies will be lined up ready to go to battle, however an extreme weather change, of rain and hail, will take them by surprise. Their plans turn to an alternative plan and they try to drop bombs on the opposing forces.

News; unexpected and heavy rain
will suddenly prevent two armies.
Stones and fire from the skies will make a sea of stones.
The death of the seven suddenly by land and sea. (ΙΙ-18)

Somehow through the espionage powers of the Anti-Christ, they will be discovered and destroyed. This on the one hand will help him because it will throw the organizations he’s opposing into temporary disorder and he can take advantage of this chaos. But on the other hand, it’s a little bit shortsighted of him because it’s this cabal that has been instigating the warfare that has been going on through the decades and centuries. His destroying them will in effect write the beginning of the end for him.
