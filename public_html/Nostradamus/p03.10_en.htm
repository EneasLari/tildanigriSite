3.10 U.S.A, inexhaustible source of global events

A fox will be elected without speaking one word,
appearing saintly in public living on barley bread,
afterwards he will suddenly become a tyrant
putting his foot on the throats of the greatest men. (VIII-41)

President George Bush will create an unstable situation for his country; during his reign it can be a turning point of United States history. Just like «a fox» that «will be elected without speaking one word,» he has been able to observe over a span of many years how the leader of the Chinese government manipulated the masses, and is implementing it to the extent he can …


He will enter, wicked, unpleasant, infamous,
tyrannizing over Mesopotamia.
All friends made by the adulterous lady,
the land dreadful and black of aspect. (VIII-70)

The American invasion to «Mesopotamia» – ancient name for Iraq – the statue of Liberty will be «the adulterous lady» since liberty has been whored out in a dreadful country such as the United States. She is also "adulterous" because she wouldn’t be true to her allies at this point in your time.  


The copies of gold and silver inflated,
which after the theft were thrown into the lake, at the discovery
that all is exhausted and dissipated by the debt.
All scrips and bonds will be wiped out. (VIII-28)

It will be a great unrest in the United States because of some unscrupulous government manipulations that will create a horrible disaster in economy. Hopefully the country itself will survive but the people in power will be broken down.


The kingdom stripped of its forces by fraud,
the fleet blockaded, passages for the spy;
two false friends will come to rally
to awaken hatred for a long time dormant. (VII-33)

The situation will lead to a new American Civil War; it will be a country divided unto itself.

Treasure is placed in a temple by Western citizens
withdrawn therein to a secret place,
the temple to open by hungry bonds,
recaptured, ravished, a terrible prey in the midst. (Χ-81)

This quatrain has also to do with corruption within the government and how this rich nation is not taking care of its own people, its own environment. There are many problems they can’t control and take care of and cleanse. There is much corruption, greed and control so a few get wealthy and fat. But there’ll be a cleansing such as this country has never seen before. It will be shocking.

 «The treasure is placed in a temple by Western citizens. Withdrawn therein to a secret place» means it is something that is unknown to the average person. When the facts of corruption and greed come to light, - «the temple to open by hungry bonds, recaptured, ravished, a terrible prey in the midst» – it seems like many resignations, as if the party in power will fall. This will be unique in its history; it will bring in a temporary government. It will be the first time somebody who ranks in the third or fourth position will be called in to take over.
