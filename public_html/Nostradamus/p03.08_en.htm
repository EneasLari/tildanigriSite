3.8 Massive emigration from Albania to Greece


At the great market, called that of the liars,
of all Torrent and the field of Athens:
they will be surprised by the light armed horses, by the Albanians,
when Mars is in Leo, and Saturn in Aquarius. (V-91)


Major shake-up is expected in the European Common Market – «the great marketcalled that of the liars» - and will particularly concern some eastern-bloc nations. There will be a general upheaval in the Balkans as well as massive immigration mainly by the Albanian people to Greece – «the field of Athens». «The light armed horses» means that the issues will turn out to be a lot more major than people at first thought.
