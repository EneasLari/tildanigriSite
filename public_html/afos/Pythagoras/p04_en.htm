Number One

As these incorporeal forms and first principles could not be expressed in words, they had recourse to demonstration by numbers. Number One denoted to them the reason of Unity, Identity, Equality, the purpose of friendship, sympathy, and conversation of the Universe, which results from persistence in Sameness. For unity in the details harmonises all the parts of a whole, as by the participation of the First Cause.
