on Visions and Grace
   

"The Grace of God grants us a state of Joy, Serenity, Equanimity. Then, it isn't necessary for us to have Visions and Experiences, particularly if we are of those who talk about them... For if a Vision or a Message beyond this Earth is true, our mind, our thought, our tongue, lack the capacity to describe in words what it was or what has taken place... Only our life changes, and the others notice the change.."
