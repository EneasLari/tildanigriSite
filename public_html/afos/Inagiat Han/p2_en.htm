The lover of God, not the student of His nature

When man analyses the objective world and realizes the inner being, what he learns first and last is that this whole vision of life is created of love; love itself being life, all will in time be absorbed in it.

It is the lover of God whose heart is filled with devotion, who can commune with God; not the one who makes an effort with his intellect to analyse God. In other words, it is the lover of God who can commune with Him, not the student of His nature. It is the "I" and "you" which divide and yet it is "I" and "you" which are the necessary conditions of love. Although "I" and "you" divide the one life into two, it is love that connects them by the current which is established between them; and it is this current which is called communion, which runs between man and God.

To the question, "What is God?" and "What is man?" the answer is that the soul, conscious of its limited existence, is "man", and the soul reflected by the vision of the unlimited, is "God".
