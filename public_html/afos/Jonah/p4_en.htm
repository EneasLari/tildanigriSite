We, the ancient Essenes, return

(...) We are not here to represent man's religion. We are here to fulfill a prophecy to return and to offer a message. For we well understand that those that have eyes to see, will see; those that have ears to hear will hear; those that don't, will not. It is always personal choice.

But yet, it has been our purpose to return since we were last here in what you would term "two of your thousand years ago", in the time of the Essenes. We gave forth the prophecy then that we would return again to assist humanity in the shift from one age unto another age of deeper awareness, of true spirituality, where humanity will cease placing spirituality outside of itself but begin claiming spirituality within one's own self.
