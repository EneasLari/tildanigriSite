"Meditations"

This is something most marvelous ...

This is something most marvelous if you come upon it.

I can go into it, but the description is not the described. It's for you to learn all this by looking at yourself - no book, no teacher can teach you about this.

Don't depend on anyone, don't join spiritual organizations; one has to learn all this out of oneself. And there the mind will discover things that are incredible. But for that, there must be no fragmentation and therefore immense stability, swiftness, mobility.

To such a mind there is no time and therefore living has quite a different meaning.
