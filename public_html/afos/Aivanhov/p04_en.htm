"The Powers of Thought"

The almighty instrument of thought

Thought is a force, a power, an instrument given to man by God, so that he, may be a creator in the same way that God is a creator: in beauty and perfection. By means of his thought, man can make contact with all kinds of different substances, quintessences and creatures in either the divine or the infernal regions, and if he does not know that thought is creative, he may easily get involved in such negative, disintegrating preoccupations that he destroys himself.
