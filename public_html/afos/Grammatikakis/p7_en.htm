The Autobiography of Light

The past surrounds us

(...)
It should be noted that the revelation of the past has reached today back to the "infancy" of the Universe, only a few hundred thousand years after the Big Bang. Indeed, the special satellite, which as we saw was launched into Space to detect the microwave radiation, captured some subtle undulations, some "wrinkles" in its isotropic structure. These wrinkles reflect the slow but steady formation of galaxies.  

So, in the primordial light the fingerprints of the eternally Absent. were found. Man is not allowed to see his own Face. It is a limit into the extreme past, raised by the light again; since before the formation of galaxies, the radiation of light is trapped in its constant conflict with matter. The Great Beginning will probably remain accessible to the mind, but not to the senses and our technological achievements.
