January 11, 1998

 

Look at the Moon and recognize within it the Light reflected. Just as Adam granted one of his ribs for woman to be created, likewise the Moon was cut from the body of the Sun to reflect its Light, even when the night hides It from the eyes of his children. And as the Moon is illuminated by another source, likewise woman carries the light of her husband into the world. He grants her the Light that sows the new seeds, the Light that impregnates her.

The Master
