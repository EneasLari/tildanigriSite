16. Light, riddled with bullets ...

   

The smallest things grow when our expectations from others are many ... Here, we are alone, no matter how much we believe we have company ... "I remember" when I came and I know the reality of my solitude when I leave.

All the rest is delightful breaks in order to endure the journey which is by far the most difficult thing the divine will has devised ...

That's why I can't stand it when people condemn acts and attitudes.

If we all considered the weight of each one's journey and his power of endurance, we would see that his behaviour is his capacity to defend himself. Outrageous sometimes, counter-productive for his life, but still his very own; that's why the hard lessons are for him alone. It's only natural of course that the bullets wound those around him, but the plan works circularly, anyway ...

"Worthy" people are riddled with bullets, that's why they have become lighter and they can leave and see the real truth which we are all seeking ...ï¿½ The more we ache the more we rise to the peaks we aimed for, of course unconsciously ... when I'm able to feel that way, I dissolve out of love for the people and fully justify them.
