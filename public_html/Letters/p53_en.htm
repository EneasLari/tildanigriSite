53. When shall we be Love

 

TEXT Title

No matter how dark it is around us, the flashes of light all around, show us the possibility of the Light. We are just before the final end of earthly emotions. Afterwards Love covers all the other emotions with its wings and super brilliant is born. Because when we are Love, no other emotion will fit into It. Emotions exist before love ... love is not an emotion, it is pure nature ... It is heaven ... it is a vision ... How close we are really!

I don't know what you are for everyone else ... but I know what you are for me ... In my most difficult hours you were the light that broke up the dark which surrounded me ... When everything blackened  under the shadow of events, you softened them with the right words you always said to me ... You were the presence that filled the gap after so many absences which broke my heart ... In my most difficult hours, an oasis was your company, a medicine were your words and courage was the  love we felt for one another ...


I've always known you, I just didn't believe the amount of joy I felt when our paths crossed. You were one of these gifts which Gods offer only to good children, that's why I'm grateful and I thank them. And I thank you too, because you wished to cross my path again. And life is worth it only for this, no matter how bitter it may be ...
