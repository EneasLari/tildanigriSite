19. The vision of the Light heals my soul

 



A sky full of rainbows. I see the details of the sunrays in the sky over verdant mountains behind us. We're on the beach again. Up till now, only once in my life I remember seeing two rainbows appearing simultaneously, after torrential rain. Now, the sky is filled with the wonderful seven-coloured spectrum of sunlight.

If someone raises their eyes to the sky and sees this spectacle, how can they remain the same?

The vision of the Light heals my soul.

The Master says "Whoever saw once, can never close his eyes again, because the Light is the End and the Beginning, the Eternal and the Present, the Existence."
