54. The fireworks remind us of the spiritual world



September 2003. We're joking around with "P", a friend, being in a generally teasing mood. Over her seventh energy centre I see small spherules, almost like dots, golden, mauve, blue, red and golden ones again, all sparkling and vibrating simultaneously. I literally see her joy.

When we see balloons and fireworks in our everyday life, we're delighted, because they subconsciously remind us of the spiritual world. They awaken the memory of the wonderful feeling the soul has, when it sees the "images" of the spiritual world.
