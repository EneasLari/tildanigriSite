26. The portrait of another friend

 


This is a friend, with the dominant colours of her aura. What looks like candy, is a light colour, a pastel pink and light-blue.

She hasn't believed yet how nice she is within.

Master Rampa says that the specific pastel pink colour when it appears in the aura of people who are at a higher level of development, tells of a percentage of immaturity. One can compare it with school performance, where a student is near graduation but still has gaps and weaknesses in a particular subject or subjects.
