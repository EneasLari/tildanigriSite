41. I see the aura of a friend

 



It's summer of 2002. In the freshly painted workplace of a friend, I observe her aura. The first band which is on her skin is light blue, the classic light blue-turquoise colour that water has, which contains some green as well. The colour is displayed in the sketch almost as it really is. The band is very wide, compared to others I have seen and in relation to her body it's bright and followed by another strip of golden light, equally wide.

The halo this friend emits is a pink cloud that has filled the entire room. The friend is sweet, tender and spontaneous.
