31. Questions and answers on what I see

 



These are the colours, as I see them in my house... What exactly is this that I see?

When I look out of focus, with my peripheral vision, I see bands of blue and mauve light, touching every object from one side and orange and yellow ones on the other.

When I look straight ahead, focused and concentrated, I see the dominant colour of my aura, a violet light, which dominates in the room.

When I concentrate to see a person's aura, with my peripheral vision I see the light bands as I see them in the sketched objects. However, in order to see the dominant colour of his aura, I must look straight ahead, focus and concentrate on him. Then the light he carries emerges, as if being illuminated, and his colour is revealed
