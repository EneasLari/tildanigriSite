37. Love will fill everything

 

In the ceaseless transformation of the light I see...

5 May 2002. A sunset in the countryside. I see the above picture with open eyes. I close them and what is painted in the picture below is formed. The green of fresh grass is the light of Love ceaselessly sent by the Lord to Earth. On Earth the transformed light vibrates into the pink of love.

Love will fill everything. Love will fill my heart.
