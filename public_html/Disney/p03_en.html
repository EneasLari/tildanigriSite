3. And we lived happily ever after...


It isn't only through child's play ...
The soul draws its nourishment from
myths, legends, fairy tales ...
the language of symbols always guides us
towards the unsurpassable truth.
These symbols will become a silent guide
for the future steps of our life.
How much we need these in our earthly wanderings!

...


Tales ...
a consolation for our difficult childhood years.
Underflowing streams
of hidden codes that carry the truth,
take us to the place we are born
and to where we will return.
Our soul is thirsty for a good tale,
especially just before we fall asleep;
that's the time we get ready to leave our body
as well as earth's frequency,
to enter vast worlds, the real ones.
The key to this transition is a tale ...
