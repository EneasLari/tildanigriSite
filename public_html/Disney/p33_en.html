33. The spirit is only love ...


Yes indeed, there are invisible seeds.
It is those infused in our souls
by our Masters.
Seeds of knowledge, seeds of awakening,
seeds of patience.
Invisible seeds with visible results,
when they sprout.

...

Wise men give advice without getting paid. Because the salvation
of the soul is not a profession, neither can someone make a career
by teaching the divine knowledge (!)
Just think that Socrates, who paid with his life for the fact
that he didn't take any money, supported exactly this:
young people should not leave their friends and the free discussions,
which draw the knowledge from within them, and pay
for the knowledge of the "specialists" ...
Just imagine Jesus charging for His teachings ...
What a profitable and miserable profession!
