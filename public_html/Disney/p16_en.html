16. Say it dude!...



Work is whatever makes us feel complete.
Work is the path to freedom
and compulsion is the road to slavery.
We never work with internal pressure
because then we may produce negative energy.
Our talent is the road sign
on the journey of our life; it will lead us
to our dream, to our predetermined destination.

...





Passion alone is not enough, knowledge and work
is necessary for us to bring results.
Unbridled passion "tarnishes" our goal.
Think of an actor who cannot enunciate
out of excessive passion.
The gift alone carries no weight.
We must seek work;
work of the spirit and soul as well as of the body.
We should not neglect the work required
by the circumstances of Earth.
