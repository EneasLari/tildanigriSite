34. My imagination runs wild, so do I!


So, you see our desires are the driving force of life,
the instinct that ensures our development.
Reality lives inside our imagination,
hence we constantly cultivate it.

...

With imagination,
we do not create something
that does not exist.
On the contrary,
we tune in with something
that already exists.
Our magical world:
everything is possible,
as long as there's hope!

...

Here is the power of imagination: it gives us the picture right away!
It warns and prevents us where the mind or the will cannot guide us,
or even more often get us into trouble with their "miscalculations".
