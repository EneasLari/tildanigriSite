32. So, finally, is Faith in our DNA?


Well, how about us!
Even when we do not believe, we do believe!
Faith is in our cells, because it is
the knowledge we have collected drop by drop,
experience by experience, throughout our lives.
Sometimes, mind will poison it with doubt
- after all we are only human -
but still deep inside we remain unshakable.

...

We have confidence in immediate Providence ... just when
it's needed, divine Will shall order and bring forth whatever
is necessary. We neither need to provide nor store,
and regarding this it's true that we exaggerate,
because we are overwhelmed by insecurity.
