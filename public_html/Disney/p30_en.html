30. Awakening

Awakening, the opening of the soul to universal Love,
makes us become lyrical and inventive:
Donald describes his happiness and creates
a modern use of the parable: he compares his new situation
- awakening - with the utilitarian capacity of his camera:
"I have found the secret of success: the lens of fate opened
and revealed the great truth about the darkroom of life to me".

Likewise in awakening, man manages to see through the veil of oblivion and the fog covering his eyes.
The heart is activated and crushes delusion; and that's "the secret of success". All the truths that
a person experienced in his lives have finally become comprehensible in the heart.
