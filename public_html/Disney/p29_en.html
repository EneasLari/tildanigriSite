29. Ah, love ...


We sing our love ...
Love is the divine force inside our body.
Some say that love is a sin or that
it does not comply with spirituality.
Do not ever listen to them because
the exact opposite is valid:
no one can become a spiritual person
if he does not pass through the sexual route,
because spiritual energy is itself
actually transformed erotic energy.
So, when we feel that we are in love,
it's as if our conscience expands
and we can face problems,
which previously appeared insurmountable.

...


We surrender to passionate love and to love
and we let ourselves go in their power
with the whole of our soul. We study
how to connect the cables of erotic energy
in the University of life here on Earth.
That is why we fall in love with passion;
because in that way only shall we learn
to make correct circuits without short circuiting.
Finally, we discover the simple and deep secret,
which was already known to us:
the material that protects the lovers each time
and smoothly turns the energy to the receiver,
is love, and this whole journey through all this falling
in love has this purpose: within ourselves,
to one day be flooded
with the revealing and magnificent Love.

...



Hey children, do external appearances play any role?
No, not at all when it comes to love!!!
Each one sees the radiation of the soul in each other,
the identity of his/her soul as it is emanated,
that's why attraction remains inexplicable up until nowadays
and people say "it's chemistry, it's chemistry ..." It is the
youth of the soul that colours the body with internal beauty.

...


Besides,
everyone will find
his mate without fail,
even if it is necessary
to go to another planet.

...


We are not short of humour ...
we weave verses with subversive content
and sing them, indeed we astonish our mate...
Because we are unpredictable,
who can categorize us,
"fit" us into ready-made moulds;

We have humour in life and in love, because ...
humour affects the soul in a similar way as love does!
Because love makes you more available to recognize things,
to difference, to nuances and understanding.
Just as it happens to a small kid who is surprised by his daily discoveries
and this puts him in the mood to play and laugh.

Humour is the momentary reversal of the earthly time-space,
the form and all the limitations of man.
This essence of the reversal contained in the joke,
frees the soul as it suddenly coordinates it with its frequency.
As it is natural, the soul feels bliss, such a strong feeling
that manages to shake the body and make it
vibrate and sound out of pleasure, with laughter.
While we laugh, the self is laid bare from the clothing of pretence
and for a short while
it is in the non-dimensional, time-less and non-space situation.

...



When did you last have a Love test?
Because Love says that when one asks for
forgiveness from the bottom of his heart,
the other forgives him immediately!
Because love has broadened his heart
so much as to for-give one more
behaviour which he could not tolerate
until yesterday.

...


Our soul comes to Earth with the desire
of eternal love. When we listen
to the voice of our heart we find this love and
then we feel that our destiny has been fulfilled.
Destiny is none other than the predestined life plan
of every human being, which certainly includes love.
That is why whoever feels it,
believes that fate has spoken.

...


The souls that truly love each other, cannot separate because this is simply impossible;
there is no Separation in the universe: the eternal example is Donald and Daisy,
who constantly separate in their earthly relationship, but are together forever and ever!
