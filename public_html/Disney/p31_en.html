31. How to become "super"!...


The duty of the junior woodchucks
is to help everyone who is in danger.
But super-Goofy also runs to offer
assistance - this is his mission.

...

When you have reached the point where you constantly do good
and help your fellow beings, then God gives you supernatural powers,
meaning those of the soul:
you become like super-Goofy, Phantom Duck and many others.
In other words, he will take you into His team, Spiritual category A,
and will use you as a channel for His Voice
to pass through and His therapeutic energy towards people.
You will become a co-worker in the divine Plan,
something like a graduate student to whom the professor
gives some initiatives to participate in the work of the governing board.
