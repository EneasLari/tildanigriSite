20. Give me a sign ...




In difficult and dead-end moments
we ask for a sign from Heaven.
The inventiveness of the universe as well as
the power of the message are such that the signal
shakes our heart and activates us.
If we are not sure, then it is not a sign from God,
but rather a fabrication of our mind.
Look how Mickey went crazy,
when he found the very Mickey Mouse magazine
in which he plays the leading role!
...


Children, let's ask with all the power of our soul
from our guides, to leave us signs on our road,
because these are the words of the Angels ...
it is a brief contact with the Other Side,
a wink, an agreement with the universe.
In every query concerning our course,
the universe confirms or prevents:
the safest guide to our explorations.
