9. Cooperation makes me stronger
Who would have expected this from Scrooge?
And yet, this is what we place above everything else:
we love our friends and fellow human beings
and we don't hesitate to deprive ourselves
of our most valued belonging, for the common good.
God's beloved people do not feel right
enjoying the gifts of life by themselves;
they want to share.
...

With "pooled resources" and the proper functioning
of team work, we can achieve so much more.
We come from a world of cooperation
and we will seek this here on Earth as well.
We open ourselves to others.
Cooperation shields our effort with a strong aura
and protects us from the feelings of selfishness
and separativeness.
