27. Ideaaa!!!


Where do we "download" an idea from? Where does thought come from?
Who sends it? Cosmic rays of thought reach the top of our head like a soft touch.
When the energy of the "idea" meets ours, a light switches on above our heads.

We download thoughts from the Source of universal knowledge just as
we download whatever we need from the Internet. We surf with our consciousness
in the upper energy layers, where entities of the spiritual world live,
which we call thoughts and we grasp a thought as follows: the mind relaxes,
gives space to the heart and the energy is formatted into a thought.
Then, we launch the thought at lower levels - in our conscious -
(as if it were a ship!) and work it out.

through it and thus the mechanism of thought is activated within him.

and so a person's mind can conceive innovative ideas in inventions,
arts, but also inner insights for his evolution.

Gyro connects with his higher Self, who as we said
"is illuminated" in the seventh centre every time
an idea is "downloaded".
But he also connects with other wise men,
invisible spiritual forces for our earthly eyes, guides
and Masters, who implant knowledge in people's minds.
We, the people of earth, are messengers
and guardians of the Light.

of our earthly eyes. Donald challenged Huey to discover something,

and so the light went on! But even generally, when the solution to the problem

his sensors what had happened there... God enlighten us!

Telepathic communication - the language of the souls -
is possible through concentration.
The main instigator of thought transmittance is the heart.
Ineffable thought is stronger because it remains
free from form and language restrictions.
It is pure essence.
But also anyone who wants to be receptive
to mental transmittances must constantly live his life
with dedication, concentration and crystal clarity;
a single moment's concentration is not enough.
This kind of concentration reminds us of wanting
to make an impression and jugglery.
Let us commit ourselves to something more stable,
and let us enjoy exercises in difficulty.
